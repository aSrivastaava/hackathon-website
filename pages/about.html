About <Code Break/>
Codebreak is a hackathon where students from all over the nation would participate. In this hackathon, participants will solve any one of the given problem statements within the span of 26 hours. The best solution will be awarded as winner by a panel of experts. The hackathon will be organized in collaboration with “Devfolio”.
